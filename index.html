Narendra Modi

Narendra Modi is the 14th and current Prime Minister of India, serving since May 2014 . He was born on September 17, 1950, in Vadnagar, India . Modi is a member of the Bharatiya Janata Party and the Rashtriya Swayamsevak Sangh . He was the Chief Minister of Gujarat from 2001 to 2014, during which he was credited for the rapid growth of the state's economy . Modi is known for his economic reforms and development-oriented leadership style

Modi's tenure as Prime Minister has been marked by several initiatives, including the Swachh Bharat Abhiyan (Clean India Mission), Make in India, Digital India, and Skill India He has also launched several welfare schemes, such as the Pradhan Mantri Jan Dhan Yojana, Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana, and Ayushman Bharat Modi has been praised for his efforts to eradicate black money from India and his focus on development and good governance . He is also known for his strong online presence and use of social media to connect with people
